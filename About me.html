About me

I am Aashik Villa, a self taught artist.

I strongly believe art is lot more than just a hobby and is no less than any other mainstream proffession. A lot can be done if you are passionate about the work you do."DO WHAT YOU LOVE"

In 2018 I created an Instagram page presenting my artwork. I feel blessed to know that my sketches have been loved by so many people that i started making commission potraits from 2019.

Starting from the first post to the last you can see my improvements. Of course, you will get an idea of how much do I love my work.It has been a great journey.

As the years passed I focused more on realism.YouTube and Instagram has been a great source of inspiration and learning.
